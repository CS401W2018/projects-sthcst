Why do we need a viewport tag?

Without a viewport tag, mobile browsers will render the page at a typical desktop screen width, and then scale the page down to fit the device's screen. This can make the page difficult to read and use on a small screen.

When you select the same tag twice, and in one of them you say "flex-direction: column;" and the other says "flex-direction: row;", which one takes priority?

The last one takes priority.
