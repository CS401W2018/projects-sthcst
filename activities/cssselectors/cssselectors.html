What is the difference between an ID and a Class?

An ID is like a variable that you cannot reuse or assign to multiple tags. IDs are usually nouns. On the other hand, a class is an adjective and you can reuse it.

What is the difference between a SPAN Tag and a DIV Tag?

The difference is first, the DIV goes inside a box when tagged and takes up the whole line in the screen, and the SPAN just edits the text without changing the layout of the site.

What is the difference between a DIV tag and HEADER, MAIN, FOOTER?

Header, Main, Footer are all under the DIV Tag. DIV tags are just a container; a generic one. While the Header, Main, and Footer are used to have a structure inside the whole code.
