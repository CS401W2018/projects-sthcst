The Title and Topic of your Site

Rising Eagle Construction

A brief paragraph why you chose this topic

I chose this topic because I kept on promising my Dad that I was going to make a website to promote his construction firm, and it was being pushed over for a lot of times. Thus, doing this can help him find more clients, and even help him gather more information, while also sharing his information to a lot of people.

List out the content you will have on the homepage and each of the five pages.

Homepage: A brief introduction of the company, a slideshow of the projects that they have done, and a contact form.

About Us: A brief history of the company, the services that they offer, and the team behind the company.

Services: A list of the services that they offer, and a brief description of each service.

Projects: A gallery of the projects that they have done, and a brief description of each project.

Contact: A contact form, and a map of the location of the company.
