When should you use a background image, and when should you use the IMG tag?

The img tag is used when the image plays a meaningful role in conveying information or enhancing the content of the webpage. Since it forms an integral part of the document structure, it should be accessible to all users, including those relying on screen readers. A background image is used for decorative purposes and is part of the design or layout rather than the content.
