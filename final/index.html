About the CEO

Engineer Regidor Acosta is the CEO and founder of the Rising Eagle Construction. He has been in the construction business for over 30 years. He has built many homes and buildings in the Philippines and in the United States. He is a licensed contractor in the state of California. He is a member of the National Association of Home Builders. He is also a member of the American Society of Civil Engineers. He is a graduate of the University of the Philippines with a degree in Civil Engineering.
