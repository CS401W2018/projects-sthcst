Porter Robinson

Who is Porter Robinson?

Porter Robinson is a very famous EDM Artist. Given the fact that most of his fans are in the young age, some are also kids who are depressed and mostly detained in their houses. It is very interesting how Porter catches the hearts and interests of his fans.

Porter Robinson is an American DJ and EDM Producer. He became famous because of his song "Say My Name", which reached number one on a very famous media streaming platform in the US.

Famous Songs

  1. Shelter

    "Shelter" is my favorite song, and is probably my most favorite of all time. I am pretty sure that I am not the only one who thinks this way. When this was first released in 2016, it was like a bomb. Different people from all over the world were very excited because of the mixture of two of the greatest EDM musicians ever!

  2. Everything Goes On

    This song is another favorite of mine. There were a lot of things that made this very good. One is the lyrics. Porter is known as someone who thinks of songs that are very hard to understand. In fact, there were even different groups all over the world who would gather just to talk about their own interpretations of his songs. This song in particular is very interesting because it also is a song from a video game.

  3. Sad Machine

    A fan of Porter made up a story about this song where an AI survived an apocalypse that ended the human race on Earth. There were different parts of the lyrics where the AI would feel very sad and lonely, making the music more relatable to most of his fans.
