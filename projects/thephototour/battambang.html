Krong Battambang

Battambang is a city in northwestern Cambodia. It is the capital of Battambang Province, which was founded in the 11th century. It is the leading rice-producing province of the country, and the city is known for its beautiful French colonial architecture. Battambang is also famous for its ancient temples, pagodas, and museums. The city is a popular tourist destination, with many visitors coming to see the historic sites and enjoy the local cuisine.
